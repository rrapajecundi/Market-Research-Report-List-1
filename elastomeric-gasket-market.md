<p><ul><li>Rubber</li><li>Thermoplastic Elastomers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elastomeric gasket market comprises primarily two types: rubber and thermoplastic elastomers (TPEs). Rubber gaskets, made from natural or synthetic rubber, offer excellent flexibility, durability, and resistance to temperature fluctuations, making them ideal for various industrial applications. Conversely, TPEs combine the benefits of rubber and plastics, providing enhanced recyclability and ease of processing. These gaskets are typically more lightweight and can be molded into complex shapes, catering to diverse industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
