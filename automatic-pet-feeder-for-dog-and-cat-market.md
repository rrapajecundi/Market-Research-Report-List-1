<p><p>The automatic pet feeder market offers three primary types: Gravity-based, Electronic, and Smart feeders. Gravity-based feeders use a simple mechanism that allows food to flow from a reservoir as the pet eats, ensuring a constant supply. Electronic feeders involve programmable timers and portion control, enabling pet owners to schedule specific feeding times. Smart feeders integrate technology such as Wi-Fi connectivity and mobile app control, allowing remote monitoring and adjustments, enhancing convenience for pet owners while providing tailored feeding options for pets.</p></p>
