<p><p>The tinted soft contact lenses market is categorized into three main types: visibility tint, enhancement tint, and opaque tint. Visibility tint lenses have a light tint that helps wearers see and handle them easily without altering eye color. Enhancement tint lenses intensify the natural color of the eye, providing a more vivid appearance. Opaque tint lenses are designed to completely change the eye color, offering dramatic effects for cosmetic purposes. Each type caters to different aesthetic preferences and needs.</p></p>
