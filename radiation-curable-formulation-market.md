<p><strong>The Radiation Curable Formulation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultraviolet (UV) Curing</li><li>Electron beam (EB) Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation curable formulation market comprises two primary types: Ultraviolet (UV) curing and Electron Beam (EB) curing. UV curing utilizes ultraviolet light to initiate polymerization, resulting in rapid drying and hardening of coatings, adhesives, and inks, making it suitable for various applications like printing and automotive finishes. In contrast, EB curing employs high-energy electron beams to achieve polymerization without the need for photoinitiators, offering advantages in depth of curing and heat-sensitive substrates, thereby enhancing productivity and performance in industrial processes.</p></p>
