<p><p>The CW (Continuous Wave) radar market can be categorized into single frequency and multiple frequency types. Single frequency CW radars operate on a single, constant frequency, providing simplicity and reduced cost, making them suitable for applications like speed detection. In contrast, multiple frequency CW radars utilize various frequencies, allowing for enhanced detection capabilities and improved accuracy in target discrimination. This versatility makes them ideal for complex applications, such as surveillance and tracking in dynamic environments.</p></p>
