<p><p>Digital-to-Analog Converters (DACs) transform digital signals into analog form, essential for audio, video, and control applications. The market types include R-2R DACs, which use a resistor ladder network, and String DACs that utilize a series of resistors for conversion. High-Speed Current-Steering DACs excel in speed and precision, while Delta-Sigma DACs prioritize accuracy through oversampling and noise shaping. Other technologies encompass variations like PWM and hybrid designs, each catering to specific performance and application needs across diverse industries.</p></p>
