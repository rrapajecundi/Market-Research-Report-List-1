<p><strong>The Silicone Room Temperature Glue Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Condensed Type</li><li>Addition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone room temperature glue market includes two primary types: condensed type and addition type. Condensed type silicones cure through moisture absorption and typically offer good flexibility and adhesion, making them suitable for various applications. Addition type silicones, on the other hand, cure through a chemical reaction without releasing byproducts, resulting in higher precision and durability. Both types are utilized across industries such as construction, automotive, and electronics, catering to diverse bonding and sealing needs.</p></p>
