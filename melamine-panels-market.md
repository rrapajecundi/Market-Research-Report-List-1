<p><p>Melamine panels are engineered wood products coated with a melamine resin finish, known for durability and aesthetic appeal. The market is primarily segmented into particle panels and density panels. Particle panels, made from wood particles bonded with adhesive, offer affordability and versatility for furniture and cabinetry. In contrast, density panels, often referred to as medium-density fiberboard (MDF), provide a smoother surface and greater density, making them ideal for applications requiring precision and a high-quality finish. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs in the furniture and construction industries.</p></p>
