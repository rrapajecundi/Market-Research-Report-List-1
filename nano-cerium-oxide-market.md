<p><p>The nano cerium oxide market can be categorized into two main types: dispersion and powder. Dispersion refers to nano cerium oxide particles that are suspended in a liquid medium, often used in applications like catalysts and coatings due to their enhanced surface area and reactivity. Powder form, on the other hand, consists of dry particles, used primarily in ceramics, polishing, and as a UV stabilizer. Both forms leverage the unique properties of nano cerium oxide for various industrial applications.</p></p>
